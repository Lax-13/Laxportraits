Frequently asked questions

Answers to the logistics couples, families, and creative directors ask most before booking.

How far in advance should we book?

Weddings are typically booked 6–12 months ahead. Lifestyle and branding sessions are available within 2–4 weeks.

Do you travel outside Gauteng?

Yes. Travel across South Africa and neighbouring countries is available with custom packages.

How many photos do we receive?

Weddings include 450–650 images. Lifestyle and branding sessions average 60–120 polished photographs.

Ready to tell your story?

Send through your wedding date or campaign timeline and we will reply with availability within one business day.
